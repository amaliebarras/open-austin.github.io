Every month Open Austin hosts three major events:

  • Open Gov & Civic Tech Meetup - A monthly event were we invite speakers from the civic tech community and cover a broad range of topics. This is normally at the Terrazas public library in East Austin.
  • Civic Hack Night - We try to invite a non-profit or anybody with an idea for a civic tech project to come pitch their ideas. Then we break out into project-specific groups and work for about two hours. Normally at WeWork on 6th & Congress.
  • Civic Hack Saturday - Like the Civic Hack Night but on Saturday's and without presentations. This is a comfortable space to come and work on a project for a few hours. Usually at Urban Co-Lab.
